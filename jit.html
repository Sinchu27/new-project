ABOUT US

Institute of Technology (JIT) is a renowned private engineering college located in Bangalore, Karnataka, India. The college is affiliated to the Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belgaum and approved by AICTE, New Delhi. It was established by Dr. B N V Subramanya, a Karnataka Rajyotsva awardee, and is managed by the Jyothy Charitable Trust. The college offers a Bachelor of Engineering degree in six disciplines, namely Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Information Science and Engineering. JIT is well-equipped with modern facilities and has a team of experienced faculty members who ensure that students receive a comprehensive education. The college also offers various extracurricular activities, including sports, cultural events, and technical fests, which provide students with opportunities to develop their skills and interests.
